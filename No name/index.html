Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad
with Roasted Chicken

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted
chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingrediants

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quino
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preperation

  • Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 in middle.

    • Time: 40 minutes

  • Toss quinoa, chicken, kale, and Caesar dressing until coated.

    • Time: 4 minutes

  • Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.

    • Time: 1 minute
